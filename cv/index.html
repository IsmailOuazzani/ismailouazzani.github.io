  • Sep 2022 - Dec 2022
    Delivery Turtlebot
    • In our robotics class, we designed a control system for simulating a Mail Delivery Robot. Our task was to enable the robot to navigate a topological map of an office track and stop at specific offices. We implemented Bayesian Localization and a PID controller to achieve this. By leveraging a TurtleBot3 Waffle Pi equipped with a Pi Camera, we utilized color detection and line following techniques. We successfully implemented the system and gained valuable experience in prototyping and debugging, deepening our understanding of robotics concepts.
